NeedOne — Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your NeedOne account and data at any time, directly within the app. No need to email support or fill out a separate form.

Steps to delete your account

  1. Open the NeedOne app and log in
  2. Go to the Profile tab
  3. Tap the gear icon (top right) to open Settings
  4. Scroll to the bottom and find Danger Zone
  5. Tap Delete my account
  6. Confirm twice — this step is irreversible, so we ask you to confirm on two separate prompts

What gets deleted

  • Your account and login credentials
  • Your profile — name, email, photo, bio, area, sports, and skill levels
  • Games you've posted
  • Your comments, messages, ratings, and Player of the Match votes
  • Your follower/following relationships and crew list

What's retained

Some game records you were part of may remain associated with the game itself — for example, a game you attended may still show as having had a participant, without identifying you, once your account is gone. No personally identifying information about you remains attached to it.

How long it takes

Deletion happens immediately when you confirm — there's no waiting period.
